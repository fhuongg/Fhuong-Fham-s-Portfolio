Yours truly,

My work lives somewhere between curiosity and nostalgia. I enjoy creating playful visuals, but I’m just as drawn to the quiet, imperfect details that carry emotion. I see design as a way of piecing together fragments - of texture, colors, words - into something that feels rare and personal.
When I'm not chasing ideas, I'd probably be reading feminist literature or writing poems on my notes app. Most of my projects start from an idea that feels alive, and I follow that thread until it becomes a world of its own.
